# Why an Electronic Laboratory Notebook?

## Research Data Management

Research data are valuable scientific assets. Proper documentation ensures that experimental results remain understandable and reusable years after they were generated.

---

## FAIR Principles

Research data should be

- **Findable**
- **Accessible**
- **Interoperable**
- **Reusable**

These four FAIR principles form the basis of modern research data management.

---

## European Research Landscape

European research infrastructures increasingly require structured research data management.

Funding agencies and research institutions expect

- documented workflows,
- reproducible experiments,
- standardized metadata,
- long-term availability of research data.

---

## Why NOMAD?

The NOMAD ecosystem provides

- an Electronic Laboratory Notebook
- standardized metadata
- automated parsing
- data publication
- workflow integration
- FAIR data management
